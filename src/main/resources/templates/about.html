About 9rove

9rove is a minimalist financial intelligence project. It surfaces timely, high-signal insights from public markets — focusing on IPOs, insider moves, and evolving capital activity.

We aim to reduce noise, highlight what matters, and make financial data easier to interpret at a glance.

9rove is just beginning. The current release emphasizes IPO tracking, but deeper features are growing quietly in the background.
