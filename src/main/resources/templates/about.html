About 9rove

Your lightweight window into U.S. market activity.

Mission

9rove is a lean, fast, and focused financial data site. Our mission is to provide simple yet valuable information — free of charge — to help you stay informed about key developments in U.S. equity markets.

What You’ll Find Here

  • 🆕 Recent and upcoming IPOs, sorted by exchange
  • 📄 Insider SEC disclosures and executive trades
  • 🗓️ Data-driven insights updated regularly

Our Philosophy

No flashy dashboards. No cookie-cutter content. Just clean, focused data presentation. We believe the web should be fast, elegant, and respectful of your time.

What’s Next

We’re working on additional data views — from SPAC listings and corporate actions to cross-linked financial events. PostgreSQL-powered, privacy-respecting, and API-friendly.

Feedback

Got suggestions? Let us know. We’re iterating actively, and your ideas shape what comes next.
